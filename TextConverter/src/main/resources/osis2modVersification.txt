#! In theory, the Crosswire implementation presumably follows OSIS, since I
#! was told that anything I do has to be OSIS-compliant.  Except it doesn't,
#! because Crosswire support a much more extensive set of DC books than are
#! listed in the OSIS spec.  Anyway, given that the Crosswire list is far and
#! away the most comprehensive, and given that I assume osis2mod etc follow
#! that, I have chosen to go with that.

#! which books appear in particular versification schemes, how many verses
#! they contain, etc.

#! the details for a single scheme come in a single block.  I appear to have
#! gone to some lengths where DC books are involved to follow the book ordering

#! pick up the fact that one of the schemes orders the Protestant OT canon in
#! a different manner.
